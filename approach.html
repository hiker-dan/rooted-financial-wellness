My Approach

Budgeting as
Self-Care

Before we touch a single number, we start with your relationship to money.

Most financial advice skips the most important step: understanding how you feel about money. Your spending habits, your financial fears, your learned behaviors—all of this is rooted in emotion, experience, and identity.

Gaining this clarity allows us to define your values around money. Those values are core to how we build your budget together!

The Tool

Zero-Based Budgeting, Built Together in YNAB

The foundation of everything we do is zero-based budgeting: every dollar you earn gets a purpose. No loose strands of funds floating around. The primary purpose is intentionality.

We will use YNAB (You Need A Budget)—a platform built around the zero-based budgeting philosophy. YNAB syncs with your bank accounts in real time, works on your phone and computer, and is designed to adapt as your life changes.

All four paid tiers leave the first session with a real, living budget.

YNAB Access—Included During Coaching

I want you focused on building your budget, not worrying about software costs. YNAB access is included throughout our coaching sessions.

After coaching, YNAB is $109/year (~$9/month) if you choose to continue. By then, you'll have a clear idea of what it offers. I'm not affiliated with YNAB, but it is the software I personally use for budgeting.
