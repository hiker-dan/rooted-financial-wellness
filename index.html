My Philosophy

A Different Approach to
Financial Wellness

Traditional financial advice treats money as purely logical. But the truth is, our relationship with money is deeply emotional and shaped by systemic barriers designed to keep us confused.

Emotion-First

Your money story is tied to your emotions, experiences, and identity. We start by honoring that truth, not ignoring it. Understanding your relationship with money is the first step to changing it.

System-Critical

Financial systems are intentionally complex. I help you see through the confusion and understand why managing money feels so hard. It's not you—it's by design.

Agency & Empowerment

Budgeting isn't about restriction or guilt. It's a practice of self-care and empowerment. I give you the tools and confidence to make intentional choices aligned with your values.

How It Works

Your Path to Financial Clarity

Getting started is simple. Here's what the journey looks like.

1

Free Consultation

We meet for 30 minutes to understand where you are, what you want to achieve, and whether we're a good fit. No pressure, no obligation.

2

Build Your Budget

In our first full session, we create your personalized zero-based budget together. We explore your relationship with money, set priorities, and build a plan that actually works for your life.

3

Grow & Adjust

Your budget evolves with you. Through follow-up sessions, we refine your approach, tackle new goals, and celebrate your wins along the way.
