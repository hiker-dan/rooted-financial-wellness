Apps & Tools I Recommend

These are tools I personally use or recommend to clients. I'm not affiliated with any of them—I just think they're great.

YNAB (You Need A Budget)

The gold standard for zero-based budgeting software. Powerful, intuitive, and built around the philosophy of giving every dollar a job.

Paid (free trial available)

Aspire Budgeting

A free, open-source budgeting spreadsheet for Google Sheets. Perfect if you want the zero-based approach without a subscription.

Free & open source

Google Sheets

Sometimes the simplest tool is the best. A custom spreadsheet can be all you need to track your budget effectively.
